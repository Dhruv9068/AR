About Us

At VisiARise, we are dedicated to transforming the home decor shopping experience with innovative Augmented Reality solutions. Our platform allows you to visualize and interact with furniture, artwork, and home products in real-time within your own space. We aim to make shopping more engaging, personalized, and convenient, helping you bring your design ideas to life effortlessly.

Problem Statement

Customers on Walmart's e-commerce platform struggle with visualizing products like furniture and decor in their spaces, causing hesitation and a higher rate of returns. This issue leads to decreased customer satisfaction and increased operational costs for Walmart due to product returns.

Solution

VisiARise provides AR Interface that allows customers to virtually place furniture, decor, and artwork in their real-world spaces while shopping on Walmart's e-commerce platform, By enabling users to see how products not only look but also fit in their space before purchasing, VisiARise reduces uncertainty, enhances shopping confidence, and lowers return rates, leading to improved customer satisfaction and operational efficiency for Walmart.

Future Goals

Develop AI-powered tools for more intuitive and natural user interactions in AR/VR environments. Integrate machine learning algorithms to enhance real-time object recognition and environment mapping. Create more immersive AR/VR experiences with advanced haptic feedback and sensory integration. Expand the use of AR and VR in industries like education, healthcare, and real estate for training, simulations, and virtual tours.
